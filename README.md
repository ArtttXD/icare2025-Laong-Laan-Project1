**raw \_data**

* **2024\_T15\_18.csv**  
  Table of the number of precincts, registered voters, voters who actually voted and the turnout by region.  
* **Total Population-Household\_Population-Number\_of\_Households\_2020.csv**  
  Contains the household population and number of households for all cities.  
* **Population\_age\_sex\_2020.csv**  
  Houses the population by age group, sex, region, and Province/Highly Urbanized CIty  
* **Population\_projection\_2020\_2045.csv**  
  Projected population by five-year age group and by sex and single-year interval.
